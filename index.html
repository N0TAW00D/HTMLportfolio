    About me

    Hey there! I’m Up (Natthawat Primsirikunawut), a first-year Computer Engineering student at King Mongkut's University of Technology Thonburi (KMUTT). I have a strong interest in programming and love exploring new tech that makes life easier or has the potential to impact society. Right now, I'm working on honing my coding skills and eager to learn everything that can make software more useful and user-friendly. Thanks for stopping by! Looking forward to connecting and sharing some cool ideas in the future!
